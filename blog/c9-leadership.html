How does the SCARF model apply to your Phase 0 experience so far? How will it apply to bootcamp?

Status: This has been hard for me I think I put alot into having a good job and was proud of my job. I've found it hard to
tell former work mates that I'm going back to study. But I need to remember that it's definitely not a step down in status.

Certainty: Yeah in the past being a contract worker I felt uncertainty every time our contracts came up for review, it is
debilitating. There is it feels a big leap of faith for me in this course and the career I'm heading in to. Every time I
couldn't finish something or got stuck I doubted everything I was doing.. The meditation side of this course has helped,
taking a breath focusing on goal and affirmations.

Autonomy: This one I do feel in other areas of life but through slowing everything down and focusing of what needs to be
done one step at a time is one way I've found helps with having some control in life.

Relatedness: I really liked that quote they had in the book by Warren Buffett "When you get to my age, you’ll measure
your success in life by how many of the people you want to have love you actually do love you. That’s the ultimate test of
how you’ve lived your life.”

Fairness: Yeaah I find it hard not to be like everyone else and not take the one dollar and the end up with nothing....
Though I'm also the type of person who if I had the $20 dollars to try to be fair and give them $10 but will probably think
they deserve it more and give them the whole $20... I learning to make sure my decisions benefit me as well because how can
help anyone if I can't even help myself. It's been a learning process for me this year..

How am I going to apply this to the course.
Take the last chapter in account. Realise that everyone in this course is human and that we're all going through this in one
way or another. Be empathetic and guide each other.

"Love them. Understand them. Forgive them. Grow with them."
